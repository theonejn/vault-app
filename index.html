2. Sign in with Apple ID, enable iCloud Drive

🔒 Proton Drive▶
1. Download Proton Drive from proton.me/drive
2. Default path: C:\Users\[You]\Proton Drive\
3. Create a Vault\ subfolder
4. Click Change above and select it
5. End-to-end encrypted sync
